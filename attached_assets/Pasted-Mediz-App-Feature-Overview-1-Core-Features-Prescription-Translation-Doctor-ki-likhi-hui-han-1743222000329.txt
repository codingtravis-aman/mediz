Mediz App – Feature Overview ✅ 1. Core Features 🩺 Prescription Translation: Doctor ki likhi hui handwritten prescription ko text me convert kare. Multiple languages me translation support ho. OCR (Optical Character Recognition) se handwriting detect kare. 💊 Medication Reminders: Medicine lene ke liye customizable notifications bheje. Dose timing, quantity, aur frequency set karne ka option ho. Missed dose alerts aur refill reminders bhi ho. 🔍 Medicine Information: Medicine ka name, uses, side effects, dosage, aur precautions dikhaye. Alternatives aur substitutes suggest kare. Online pharmacy se price comparison bhi show kare. 📑 Medical Reports Overview: X-ray, ultrasound, MRI, blood tests, etc. ka overview dikhaye. Reports me se key findings aur abnormal values highlight kare. Graphs aur visualizations se health trends show kare. 📤 One-Click Report Sharing: Reports ko WhatsApp, email, ya app users ke saath share karne ka option. Secure sharing with encryption. 🥗 Diet & Yoga Recommendations: Health reports ke basis par personalized diet plans suggest kare. Yoga exercises bhi recommend kare jo user ki health ke hisaab se ho. Example: Diabetes wale users ko low-sugar diet aur sugar-control yoga recommend ho. 🤖 2. AI/ML-Based Features 🧠 Brain Tumor Detection: MRI ya CT scan upload karne par tumor detection kare. Tumor ka size, location, aur severity dikhaye. Result ko visual representation ke saath show kare. 📊 Smart Health Analysis: ML models se reports ka in-depth analysis kare. Health risks (jaise diabetes, heart disease) ka prediction kare. User ko health improvement tips bhi suggest kare. 💬 AI-Powered Chatbot: Symptom checker aur basic health queries ka solution de. User ko doctor consult karne ki recommendation de. Real-time health tips bhi suggest kare. 🔥 3. Emergency & Blood Donation Features 🩸 Urgent Blood Request Notifications: Hospitals ke blood request par real-time notifications bheje. Blood group ke hisaab se targeted alerts mile. Example: O+ blood ki jarurat ho to sirf O+ wale users ko notification mile. Nearby blood banks aur hospitals ka data show kare. 🚑 Emergency Help Button: App me emergency SOS button ho jo nearest hospital se contact kare. Emergency contact ko auto-notification bheje. 🌐 4. Additional Features 📍 Nearby Clinics & Pharmacies: User ki location ke hisaab se nearby hospitals, clinics, aur pharmacies dikhaye. Navigation aur contact details bhi show kare. 💾 Health Record Backup: Cloud storage me health reports aur medical history ka backup store kare. Multiple devices se access karne ka option ho. 🌙 Dark & Light Mode: App me dark mode ka support ho for better UX. Light mode aur dark mode ke beech easy switching ho. 🔐 Data Privacy & Security: Reports aur personal data ko end-to-end encryption se secure rakhe. User ka data kisi third party ke saath share na ho. 🌟 5. Future Expansion Ideas Telemedicine Integration: App me video consultation ka option ho. Health Insurance Link: Insurance details store karne ka feature. Fitness Tracker Sync: Fitbit, Apple Health ya Google Fit se sync kare. 🔥 Yeh complete structure app ko next level banayega!
